Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Storm Studio Publisher is a private content publishing tool operated by a single individual (the "Operator") to upload short-form video content to Pages and channels owned by the Operator on third-party platforms, including Facebook Pages and YouTube channels.

1. What data the Tool stores

Storm Studio Publisher does not have end users and does not collect personal data from any third party. It only stores:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by Meta and YouTube to the Operator's own accounts, encrypted at rest on a private server controlled by the Operator.
  • Operational logs (timestamps, HTTP status codes, error traces) used for debugging. These contain no personal data of any third party.

2. Requesting deletion of your data from Meta

If you are the Operator and want Storm Studio Publisher to delete its reference to your Meta account / Pages / tokens, you can do either of the following:

  1. Go to your Facebook profile Settings → Apps and Websites → Active, find Storm Studio Publisher, and remove it. This invalidates all tokens the Tool holds for your account; the Tool loses all ability to access your data automatically and within 30 days purges any cached references during its routine cleanup.
  2. Send an email to andersonrocha.rs@live.com with the subject Data Deletion Request — Storm Studio and a description of which Page or account you want removed. The Operator will manually delete the requested data from the server within 7 days and reply to confirm.

3. Confirmation code

This page also functions as the response endpoint for Meta's Data Deletion Callback. Per Meta requirements, when a user removes this app from their Facebook account, the Operator processes the deletion and confirms it. To check the status of a specific deletion request, send an email to andersonrocha.rs@live.com with your confirmation code.
