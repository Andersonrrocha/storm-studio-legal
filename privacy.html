Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Storm Studio Publisher ("the Tool") is a private content publishing tool operated by a single individual (the "Operator") to upload short-form video content to Pages owned and controlled by the Operator on third-party platforms (such as Meta Platforms, YouTube, and similar).

1. Data we collect from end users

None. The Tool does not have end users. It is not a consumer-facing application, has no public sign-up, and does not expose any user-facing interface to third parties.

2. Data the Tool processes

The Tool processes only the following data, all of which belongs to the Operator:

  • Video files, captions, titles and descriptions authored by the Operator and stored on infrastructure controlled by the Operator.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by third-party platforms to the Operator's accounts. These tokens are stored encrypted at rest on a private server controlled by the Operator and are never shared with any third party.
  • Operational logs (timestamps, HTTP status codes, error traces) used for debugging and monitoring. Logs do not contain personal data of any third party.

3. Data we share with third parties

None, beyond the platform APIs to which the Operator publishes content (e.g. Meta Graph API, YouTube Data API). Such transfers are governed by the receiving platform's own terms.

4. Cookies and tracking

The Tool has no public web frontend and uses no cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on end users.

5. Data retention

OAuth tokens are retained for as long as the Operator's accounts on the respective platforms remain active, and revoked or deleted on demand. Logs are retained for at most 30 days and then deleted.

6. Your rights

Because the Tool processes no third-party personal data, no end-user data subject rights apply. The Operator's own data is fully under the Operator's control.
